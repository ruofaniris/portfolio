    The Class Journal

    Background

    In Chinese school, class is a concept more than people sharing the same course, but rahter a group of students who are randomly organized together according to their major through the entire studying process, regardless of differences in people's course choice. Members in a class usually share there daily life together : they fight for class glory in games, enjoy their meal time and most of them are roomates at the same time.

    In other words, a class is like a family in school, when students are far from their real home. In order to recording our precious life together, I gathered class members together to make a class journal.

    The CLass Logo

    To build up the class identity, a class logo was made to be labeled in the bottom of every page. The logo was designed based on our school logo.
