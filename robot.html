    Shopping Robot

    Background

    In order to assist people with disabilities and the aged people to buy goods in supermarkets, the concept of remote controlled shopping robot have been proposed. Such a robot is designed to provide those groups of people with an easy and comfortable shopping experience as common people enjoy.

    The remote controlled robot is required to implement the function that moving to the shelf at first and then clamping the goods, which are represented by small cubes and balls here.
