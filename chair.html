    Background

    Aiming at providing assistance to the aged people and the disabled ones who lack ability of standing up by themselves, we proposed an assistant device to aid users along the sit-to-stand process, that is, a rotatable chair cushion.

    This is a one-month course project focusing on mechanical design process, spaning from requirements analysis, to scheme research, to physical and mathematical caculation, to finite element simulation, and to 2D and 3D modelling.
