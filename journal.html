The  Class  Journal

Background

In my university, class is a concept that a group of people who are randomly organized together according to their major through the entire studying process, regardless of differences in people's course choice. Members in a class usually share their daily life together. In other words, a class is like a family in school, when students are far from their actual home.

This is a self-motivated project to recording the precious time we share together. All texts are collecting from my classmates, and I am in charge of planning and organizing the project, setting up content style, and layout design.
