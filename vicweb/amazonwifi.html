Amazon WIFI

A mobile application to provide nationwide free WIFI

About AmazonWifi

Amazon Wifi is an application we designed during the Amazon Design Jam within several hours. The theme of the design jam is to "Think Big". In that case, we design this application to provide users with free nationwide WIFI signal. By completing one of three simple tasks for Amazon, your mobile devices are able to use Amazon's nationwide free WIFI.

In this project, I served as both a UX designer and graphic designer.

Design Problem

Most of the usage Amazon's mobile application happens as a tool only for instant shopping needs. Compared to applications like Facebook, the using frequency is very limited. However, the likelihood of performing a purchasing behavior is high-positively correlated to the time a user spend in the application.

Thus, Amazon plans to improve their current mobile application to encourage customers to engage with their application in transitory moments (waiting in line, waiting/riding the bus, etc.) to boost their business.

Solution and Work Flow

Design Process

During the design jam, our team went through a quick but fairly complete design process from quick UX research, affinity wall, ideation, sketch and hi-fi prototype. In the end, we presented our complete solution to the audience and received recognition.
